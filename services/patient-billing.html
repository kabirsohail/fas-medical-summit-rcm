Why It Matters
25+ years operating in surgical specialty RCM. Real expertise, applied to your practice.

Patient billing is where revenue and reputation intersect.

The way you bill patients determines two things: how much of what they owe you actually collect, and how they feel about your practice afterward. Aggressive collections damage relationships. Passive billing damages cash flow. The right balance—firm but fair, transparent but persistent—takes craft.

Our patient billing workflow is built around clear, compassionate communication. Statements explain charges in language patients understand. Payment options are presented prominently. Payment plans are offered before delinquency happens. Multiple channels—online, phone, mail, text—make it easy for patients to pay how they prefer.

When balances do age, our follow-up is firm but professional. We don't damage the patient relationship to collect $200—the long-term cost of that approach exceeds the short-term gain. But we do collect what's owed, methodically, with the same discipline we bring to payer A/R.

Our Approach

Patient balances handled
with respect and consistency.

STEP 01

Verify what the patient owes

After insurance settles, we reconcile patient responsibility against the EOB so the statement matches what the payer actually allowed and applied.

STEP 02

Send a clear, readable statement

No cryptic abbreviations, no surprise charges. The patient knows what was billed, what insurance covered, and what they owe — in plain language.

STEP 03

Manage payment plans and questions

Patients who call get reached. Payment plans are set up, statements are explained, and disputes are routed back into the billing workflow.

STEP 04

Follow up consistently, not aggressively

A defined cadence — first statement, second, then phone outreach — replaces sporadic chasing. Better collection rates, fewer angry phone calls.
