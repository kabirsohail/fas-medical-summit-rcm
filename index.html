What We Fix

The billing problems
most practices live with.

Healthcare billing in the United States has become a discipline of its own. Complex payer rules. Aggressive denial patterns. Documentation requirements that change quarterly. We've built our practice around the three problems that cost surgical specialties the most.
01 / DELAYED REIMBURSEMENTS

Cash flow that trickles instead of flows

When payments take 45, 60, or 90 days to arrive, everything suffers—your operations, your peace of mind, your ability to plan. We architect the entire claim lifecycle to compress collection timelines through aggressive scrubbing, rapid resubmission, and disciplined payer follow-up.

02 / DENIALS & UNDERPAYMENTS

Revenue that quietly disappears

A coding error slips through. Documentation comes up short. The denial letter arrives weeks later, after staff has moved on. We catch those problems before they become your problem—and when denials do happen, we fight them with structured appeals and pattern recognition.

03 / FINANCIAL OPACITY

Reporting that reveals nothing actionable

You shouldn't need a forensic team to understand your own revenue cycle. Transparent dashboards, payer-level analytics, and quarterly executive reviews mean you know exactly where your money is and what's blocking it.

Common Questions

Everything you
need to know.

Practical answers to the questions practices ask before partnering with us. Don't see your question here? Send us a message and we'll respond as soon as we can.

What types of specialties do you support?
+
Quite a few. Our core focus is surgical specialties—ambulatory surgery centers, anesthesiology, pain management, cardiology, emergency medicine, and family practice. If your specialty has unique billing challenges (and they all do), we've probably handled something similar. Reach out and we'll tell you honestly whether we're the right fit.
How long does implementation take?
+
Depends on complexity. A straightforward practice can be fully onboarded in 2-4 weeks. Larger organizations with multiple locations or specialties typically take 6-8 weeks. We don't rush—getting the setup right matters more than hitting an arbitrary deadline.
What is your pricing / fee structure?
+
We typically work on a percentage-of-collections model—our incentives align directly with yours. Some clients prefer flat fees for specific services. We're flexible: let's have a conversation about what makes sense for your situation, then we'll build a proposal around it.
How do you ensure data security and compliance?
+
HIPAA compliance is non-negotiable. We use encrypted systems, secure access controls, regular audits, and ongoing staff training to protect patient data. We also align our operations with SOC 2 controls and provide compliance documentation on request.
How do you handle appeals and underpayments?
+
Methodically. When a claim gets denied or underpaid, we investigate why, document the appeal properly, and push back. We track patterns too—if a payer repeats the same behavior, we adjust our approach accordingly. Recovery depends on payer mix, contracts, historical underpayments, and current workflows.
Can you integrate with my current EHR / PMS?
+
In most cases, yes. We work with many major EHR and practice management systems—Epic, Athena, eClinicalWorks, NextGen, AdvancedMD, Kareo, and many others. If there is a technical hurdle, we review it during onboarding and map the practical integration path.
